  2. 2energies [plural] the physical and mental effort that you use to do something She put all her energies into her work. creative/destructive energies
  3. 3  [uncountable] a source of power, such as fuel, used for driving machines, providing heat, etc. solar/nuclear energy It is important to conserve energy. an energy crisis (= for example when fuel is not freely available) The £500 million programme is centred around energy efficiency and renewable power sources.
