Google™

 
BrE   ; NAmE  
 

a very popular Internet search engine (= a website which helps you to search for information on the Internet). The US company which runs it, Google Inc, was started in 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin. The name comes from googol, meaning the number one followed by 100 zeros.
