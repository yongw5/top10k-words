    2. 2tune something to adjust an engine so that it runs smoothly and as well as possible
    3. 3[usually passive] tune something (in) (to something) to adjust the controls on a radio or television so that you can receive a particular programme or channel The radio was tuned (in) to the BBC World Service. (informal) Stay tuned for the news coming up next.
