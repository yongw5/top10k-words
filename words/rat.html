  1. 1a small animal with a long tail, that looks like a large mouse, usually considered a pest (= an animal which is disliked because it destroys food or spreads disease) rat poison The police are all over town. He’s caught, like a rat in a trap. compare rug rat
  2. 2(informal, disapproving) an unpleasant person, especially one who is not loyal or who tricks somebody You mean he just walked out on her after fifteen years? What a rat!
