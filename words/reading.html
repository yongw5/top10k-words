Reading

 
BrE   ; NAmE  
 

a large town in southern England, governed by a unitary authority. It is famous for its music festivals, especially the Reading Rock Festival. Oscar Wilde spent two years in Reading prison (1895-7), and he wrote his Ballad of Reading Gaol there.

    8.  in parliament
