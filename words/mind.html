Mind

 
BrE   ; NAmE  
 

a British charity that works to help mentally ill people. It collects money for them, gives advice to them and their families, and aims to make people more aware of their problems. It was established in 1946 as the National Association for Mental Health, and changed its name to MIND in 1971. In the 1990s this became Mind.
