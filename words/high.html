He has climbed some of the world’s highest mountains. You also use high to describe the distance of something from the ground:How high was the plane when the engine failed? Tall is used instead of high to talk about people:My brother’s much taller than me.Tall is also used for things that are high and narrow such as trees:She ordered cold beer in a tall glass. tall factory chimneys. Buildings can be high or tall.
