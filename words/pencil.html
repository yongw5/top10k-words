She scribbled a note in pencil. coloured pencils margin notes in pencil see also eyebrow pencil, propelling pencil Word Origin Middle English (denoting a fine paintbrush): from Old French pincel, from a diminutive of Latin peniculus ‘brush’, diminutive of penis ‘tail’.
