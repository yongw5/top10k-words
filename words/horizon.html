  1. 1the horizon [singular] the furthest that you can see, where the sky seems to meet the land or the sea The sun sank below the horizon. A ship appeared on the horizon.
  2. 2[countable, usually plural] the limit of your desires, knowledge or interests She wanted to travel to broaden her horizons. The company needs new horizons now.
