    Helping others
     

  1. 1donate something (to somebody/something) to give money, food, clothes, etc. to somebody/something, especially a charity He donated thousands of pounds to charity.
  2. 2donate something (to somebody/something) to allow doctors to remove blood or a body organ in order to help somebody who needs it All donated blood is tested for HIV and other infections.
    3.  Word Origin late 18th cent.: back-formation from donation.Extra examples The company has donated more than £30 000 to local charities. There is no risk of contracting HIV through donating blood. I would like to thank our sponsors, The Woodworks, for donating the prizes for this competition.
