Wordfindercompass, globe, GPS, grid, key, latitude, map, navigate, reference, scale
  2. 2[transitive] navigate something to sail along, over or through a sea, river etc. The river became too narrow and shallow to navigate.
  3. 3[transitive] navigate something to find the right way to deal with a difficult or complicated situation We next had to navigate a complex network of committees.
