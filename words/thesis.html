  1. 1thesis (on something) a long piece of writing completed by a student as part of a university degree, based on their own research Students must submit a thesis on an agreed subject within four years.
  2. 2a statement or an opinion that is discussed in a logical way and presented with evidence in order to prove that it is true These latest findings support the thesis that sexuality is determined by nature rather than choice.
