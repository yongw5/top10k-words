    Types of restaurant, In the kitchen
     

  1. 1(British English) the part of a cooker that directs heat downwards to cook food that is placed underneath it Put it under a hot grill to brown for two minutes. compare broiler
  2. 2a flat metal frame that you put food on to cook over a fire see also barbecue
  3. 3a dish of grilled food, especially meat see also mixed grill
  4. 4(especially in names) a restaurant serving grilled food Harry’s Bar and Grill
  5. 5= grille
