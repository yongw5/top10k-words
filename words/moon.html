  1. 1  (also the moon) (also the Moon) [singular] the round object that moves around the earth once every 27½ days and shines at night by light reflected from the sun the surface of the moon a moon landing

  3. 3[countable] a natural satellite that moves around a planet other than the earth How many moons does Jupiter have?
