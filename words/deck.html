  2. 2one of the floors of a ship or a bus the upper/lower/main deck of a ship We sat on the top deck of the bus. My cabin is on deck C. see also double-decker, flight deck, single-decker

  4. 4a wooden floor that is built outside the back of a house where you can sit and relax
  5. 5a part of a sound system that records and/or plays sounds on a disc or tape a cassette/tape deck
    6.  Word Origin late Middle English: from Middle Dutch dec ‘covering, roof, cloak’, dekken ‘to cover’.
