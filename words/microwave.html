  1. 1(formal microwave oven) a type of oven that cooks or heats food very quickly using electromagnetic waves rather than heat Reheat the soup in the microwave. microwave cookery/meals compare oven
  2. 2 (specialist) an electromagnetic wave that is shorter than a radio wave but longer than a light wave
    3. Extra examples I took my lunch out of the microwave. Your dinner’s in the microwave. He lives on a diet of fast food and microwave meals. This product is unsuitable for cooking in microwave ovens. You can always reheat the soup in the microwave.
