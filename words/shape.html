    (North American English, informal) to become angry, anxious or upset Don't get bent out of shape about it. It was just a mistake!
    get (yourself) into shape
     

    to take exercise, eat healthy food, etc. in order to become physically fit I’m trying to get into shape before summer.
    get/knock/lick somebody into shape
     

    to train somebody so that they do a particular job, task, etc. well It took him just two weeks to knock the new recruits into shape.
