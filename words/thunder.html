  1. 1[intransitive] when it thunders, there is a loud noise in the sky during a storm
  2. 2[intransitive] to make a very loud deep noise synonym roar A voice thundered in my ear. thundering traffic
  3. 3[intransitive] + adv./prep. to move very fast and with a loud deep noise synonym roar Heavy trucks kept thundering past.
  4. 4[transitive] thunder something + adv./prep. (informal) to make something move somewhere very fast Essien thundered the ball past the goalie.
  5. 5[intransitive, transitive] (literary) to shout, complain, etc. very loudly and angrily thunder (something) He thundered against the evils of television. + speech ‘Sit still!’ she thundered.
