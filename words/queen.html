Queen

 
BrE   ; NAmE  
 

a successful British pop group formed in 1971. Its singer was Freddie Mercury and its albums A Night at the Opera (1975), The Game (1980) and Innuendo (1991) as well as the song Bohemian Rhapsody (1975) were all No. 1 in Britain. The 2002 musical We Will Rock You is based on the group's music.
