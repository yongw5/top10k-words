Heat the oven to a temperature of 200°C (= degrees centigrade). Some places have had temperatures in the 40s (= over 40° centigrade). see also absolute temperature, room temperature
  2. 2  the measurement of how hot somebody’s body is to take somebody’s temperature (= measure the temperature of somebody’s body using a special instrument) (British English) Does he have a temperature (= is it higher than normal, because of illness)? She's running a temperature (= it is higher than normal). He's in bed with a temperature of 40°. In North American English you can take somebody’s temperature but in the other examples the word fever is used. compare fever
