  1. 1  [uncountable, countable, usually plural] a light strong material that is produced by chemical processes and can be formed into shapes when heated. The pipes should be made of plastic. a sheet of clear plastic the plastic industry chairs made from plastic dashboards moulded in plastic
  2. 2 plastics [uncountable] the science of making plastics
