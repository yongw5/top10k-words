    Styles of music
     

  1. 1 [uncountable] a type of dance music with a strong rhythm, developed by African American musicians in the 1960s
  2. 2(also blue funk) [singular] (old-fashioned, informal) a state of fear or anxiety
  3. 3[countable, usually singular] (North American English) a strong unpleasant smell
    4.  Word Originnoun early 17th cent. (in the sense ‘musty smell’): perhaps from French dialect funkier ‘blow smoke on’, based on Latin fumus ‘smoke’.
