    [countable, uncountable] Savoury dishes
     

  1. 1fruit baked in a dish with pastry on the bottom, sides and top a slice of apple pie Help yourself to some more pie. a pie dish see also custard pie
  2. 2(especially British English) meat, vegetables, etc. baked in a dish with pastry on the bottom, sides and top a steak and kidney pie What’s the filling in these pies? see also mince pie, pork pie, shepherd’s pie
    3. Word Origin Middle English: probably the same word as pie, used in the naming of birds; the various combinations of ingredients being compared to objects randomly collected by a magpie. Idioms
