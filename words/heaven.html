    noun
    BrE BrE//ˈhevn//   ; NAmE NAmE//ˈhevn//  
    Happiness, Sky
     

  1. 1   (also Heaven) [uncountable] (used without the) (in some religions) the place believed to be the home of God where good people go when they die the kingdom of heaven I feel like I've died and gone to heaven.
  2. 2  [uncountable, countable] (informal) a place or situation in which you are very happy This isn't exactly my idea of heaven! It was heaven being away from the office for a week. The island is truly a heaven on earth.
  3. 3the heavens [plural] (literary) the sky Four tall trees stretched up to the heavens.
