  1. 1  [uncountable, countable] an act of coming or being brought to a place Guests receive dinner on/upon arrival at the hotel. We apologize for the late arrival of the train. the arrival of the mail in the morning daily arrivals of refugees There are 120 arrivals and departures every day. opposite departure
  2. 2  [countable] a person or thing that comes to a place The first arrivals at the concert got the best seats. early/late/new arrivals We're expecting a new arrival (= a baby) in the family soon.
  3. 3  [uncountable] the time when a new technology or idea is introduced the arrival of pay TV
