Ask your teacher’s advice/Ask your teacher for advice on how to prepare for the exam. Follow your doctor's advice. We were advised to seek legal advice. Let me give you a piece of advice. A word of advice. Don't wear that dress. Take my advice. Don't do it. I chose it on his advice. Why don't you/​Why not/​Could you maybe ask Tom to help? If you want my advice/​If you want to know what I think, I'd say it's better to tell him. I'd advise you to sell it now.
