  1. 1  [transitive] achieve something to succeed in reaching a particular goal, status or standard, especially by making an effort for a long time synonym attain He had finally achieved success. They could not achieve their target of less than 3% inflation.
  2. 2[transitive] achieve something to succeed in doing something or causing something to happen synonym accomplish I haven't achieved very much today. All you've achieved is to upset my parents.
  3. 3[intransitive] to be successful Their background gives them little chance of achieving at school.
