  1. 1  a person whose job involves designing and building engines, machines, roads, bridges, etc. see also chemical engineer, civil engineer, electrical engineer, lighting engineer, mechanical engineer, software engineer, sound engineer
  2. 2  a person who is trained to repair machines and electrical equipment They're sending an engineer to fix the phone.
  3. 3a person whose job is to control and repair engines, especially on a ship or an aircraft a flight engineer the chief engineer on a cruise liner
  4. 4(North American English) (British English engine driver) a person whose job is driving a railway/railroad engine
  5. 5a soldier trained to design and build military structures
    6.
