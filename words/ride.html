  3. 3  the kind of journey you make in a car, etc. a smooth/comfortable/bumpy, etc. ride (figurative) The new legislation faces a bumpy ride (= will meet with opposition and difficulties).
    4.  on horse
  4. 4  a short journey on a horse, etc. a pony ride The kids had a ride on an elephant at the zoo. He goes for a ride most mornings.
    5. at funfair
  5. 5a large machine at a funfair or amusement park that you ride on for fun or excitement; an occasion when you go on one of these The rides are free. a roller coaster ride
