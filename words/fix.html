  1. 1[countable] (informal) a solution to a problem, especially an easy or temporary one There is no quick fix for the steel industry.
    2.  drug
  2. 2[singular] (informal) an amount of something that you need and want frequently, especially an illegal drug such as heroin to get yourself a fix I need a fix of coffee before I can face the day.
    3.  difficult situation
  3. 3[singular] a difficult situation synonym mess We've got ourselves in a fix about this.
    4.  on position
  4. 4[singular] the act of finding the position of a ship or an aircraft They managed to get a fix on the yacht's position.
    5.  understanding
  5. 5[singular] (informal) an act of understanding something He tried to get a fix on the young man's motives, but he just couldn't understand him.
    6.  dishonest result
  6. 6[singular] (informal) a thing that is dishonestly arranged; a trick Her promotion was a fix, I'm sure!
