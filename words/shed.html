  2. 2shed something (formal) to let something fall; to drop something Luke shed his clothes onto the floor. A duck's feathers shed water immediately.
  3. 3shed something (British English) (of a vehicle) to lose or drop what it is carrying The traffic jam was caused by a lorry shedding its load.
    4.  skin/leaves
