  2. 2  [uncountable, countable] the pressure that is put on something when a physical force stretches, pushes, or pulls it The rope broke under the strain.   5. 5[countable, usually singular] a particular tendency in the character of a person or group, or a quality in their manner synonym streak He had a definite strain of snobbery in him.

  6. 6[countable, usually plural] (formal) the sound of music being played or sung She could hear the strains of Mozart through the window.
