    in sync
     

  1. 1moving or working at exactly the same time and speed as somebody/something else The soundtrack is not in sync with the picture.
  2. 2in agreement with somebody/something; working well with somebody/something His opinions were in sync with those of his colleagues.
    3.
    out of sync
     

  1. 1not moving or working at exactly the same time and speed as somebody/something else
  2. 2not in agreement with somebody/something; not working well with somebody/something
